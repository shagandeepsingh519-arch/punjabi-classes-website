Sat Sri Akal! — ਸਤਿ ਸ੍ਰੀ ਅਕਾਲ

We teach Punjabi (Gurmukhi) to children using short live lessons, songs, stories and worksheets. Classes are friendly and paced for young learners.

We include path practice if families request it. Schedule and monthly fee are discussed individually via email or call.
